Rosół

Description

Rosół is a traditional Polish soup based primarily on meat broth.

Ingredients

  • Chicken on the bone
  • Beef or veal bone
  • Tablespoon of vinegar
  • Vegetables
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. You have to use chicken on the bone (it can be a carcass, whole legs, thighs, drumstick, or breast; the important part is that it’s on the bone)
  2. You have to add a small amount of beef or veal bone (for extra flavor), but you can’t add pork bone, otherwise, your broth won’t be clear
  3. You should use a tablespoon of vinegar (apple cider or otherwise) to extract more of the healing power from the bones
